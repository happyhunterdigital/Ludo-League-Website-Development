📜 Our Journey

League History

From humble beginnings in Mamelodi to the biggest township Ludo showdowns in South Africa.

2022

Battle of the Kasis — The Trilogy

For the first time, three townships collide. Alexandra, Soweto, and Mamelodi face off at Dlamini Multi-Purpose Hall with R20,000 on the line. Unfortunately postponed due to logistical challenges.

📍 Dlamini Hall, Soweto💰 R20,000 Prize Pool
2021

Battle of the Kasis — The Rematch

Soweto returned to defend their crown against Alexandra at Eastbank Hall. The second edition exceeded all expectations with packed crowds and intense final matches. Soweto ran out ultimate winners.

📍 Eastbank Hall, Alexandra🎟️ Free Entry (Social Media)
2020

Mamelodi "Winner Takes All"

Post-lockdown return at Ikageng Community Hall. R10,000 winner-takes-all format brought out the most competitive field yet. The tournament proved Ludo was more than just a pastime — it was a sport.

📍 Ikageng Community Hall💰 R10,000 Prize
2019

The Beginning — Mamelodi Ludo Tournament

Where it all started. C4 Cricket Pitch in Mamelodi West hosted the inaugural tournament. 20-year-old Keamogetswe Mdawe beat 5 players on his way to the final and walked away with R10,000.

📍 C4 Cricket Pitch, Mamelodi🏆 Kea Mdawe — Champion
